## 1. Quantum Computing for Chemistry

<Definition term="Quantum Advantage">
The point at which quantum computers can solve chemistry problems faster or more accurately than any classical computer, potentially enabling calculations currently impossible with classical hardware.
</Definition>

```python

def quantum_computing():
    """Quantum computing for chemistry overview."""

    print("Quantum Computing for Chemistry")
    print("=" * 60)

    print("\nWhy Quantum for Chemistry?")
```
