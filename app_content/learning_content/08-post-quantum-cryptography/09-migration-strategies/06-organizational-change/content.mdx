## Summary

Organizational change management is critical for PQC success:

1. **People**: Training, skills development, awareness
2. **Communication**: Tailored messages for each audience
3. **Governance**: Clear roles, responsibilities, decision-making
4. **Sustainability**: Policy updates, process integration
5. **Challenges**: Address resistance, skill gaps, priorities

Technology alone doesn't create change—people do.
