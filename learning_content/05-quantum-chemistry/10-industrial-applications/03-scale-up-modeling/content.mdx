## 1. Scale-Up Challenges

<Definition term="Scale-Up">
The process of transferring a chemical synthesis from laboratory scale (grams) to pilot (kilograms) and manufacturing scale (tonnes), while maintaining product quality, yield, and safety.
</Definition>


## 2. Mass and Heat Transfer

```python
def transport_effects():
    """Modeling transport effects at scale."""

    print("Transport Effects in Scale-Up")
    print("=" * 60)

    print("\nDamköhler Number:")
    print("-" * 45)
    print("  Da = k × τ_reaction / τ_mixing")
    print()
    print("  Da << 1: Kinetically controlled")
    print("           → Lab kinetics transfer directly")
    print()
    print("  Da >> 1: Transport controlled")
    print("           → Mixing/diffusion determines rate")
    print()
    print("  DFT provides k, engineering gives τ values")

    print("\nHatta Number (Gas-Liquid):")
    print("-" * 45)
    print("  Ha = √(k × D) / kL")
    print()
    print("  Ha < 0.3: Slow reaction, bulk reaction")
    print("  Ha > 3: Fast reaction, film reaction")
    print()
    print("  DFT for k, literature for D and kL")

    print("\nHeat Transfer Analysis:")
    print("-" * 45)
    print("  Lab (100 mL flask):")
    print("    A/V = 300 m²/m³")
    print("    Heat removal: easy")
    print()
    print("  Production (10 m³ reactor):")
    print("    A/V = 3 m²/m³")
    print("    Heat removal: 100x harder!")
    print()
    print("  Computation: ΔH_rxn from DFT")
    print("  Engineering: UA coefficient")
    print()
    print("  Max heat generation rate = UA × ΔT_max")

    print("\nCFD Integration:")
    print("-" * 45)
    print("  QC provides: Rate constants, enthalpies")
    print("  CFD provides: Velocity, concentration fields")
    print()
    print("  Combined: Predict local conversion")
    print("            Identify hotspots")
    print("            Optimize mixing")

transport_effects()
```

<Formula name="Damköhler Number">
$$Da = \frac{k \cdot \tau_{reaction}}{\tau_{mixing}} = \frac{\text{reaction rate}}{\text{mixing rate}}$$

Determines whether kinetics or transport controls the reaction.
</Formula>
