## Summary

The NIST PQC standardization journey spanned 8 years with critical milestones:

1. **2016**: Call for proposals issued
2. **2017**: 82 submissions received
3. **2019**: Round 2 (26 algorithms)
4. **2020**: Round 3 (15 algorithms)
5. **2022**: Selections announced; Rainbow and SIKE broken
6. **2024**: Final standards (FIPS 203, 204, 205) published

## Key Takeaways

- The process took 8 years from call to final standards
- Algorithm count reduced from 82 to 3 final standards
- Critical breaks (Rainbow, SIKE) occurred even in late rounds
- FALCON standardization delayed due to implementation complexity
- Future milestones include FN-DSA, Round 4 decisions, and adoption mandates
