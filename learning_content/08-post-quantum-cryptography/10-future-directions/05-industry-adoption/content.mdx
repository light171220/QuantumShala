## Summary

Industry PQC adoption is accelerating:

1. **Tech Leaders**: Google, Apple, Cloudflare already deploying
2. **Cloud Providers**: AWS, Google Cloud offering PQC options
3. **Industries**: Financial services, government leading
4. **Open Source**: Mature libraries available (liboqs, pqcrypto)
5. **Vendor Evaluation**: Assess roadmaps, require commitments
6. **Approach**: Everyone using hybrid during transition

Follow early adopters: start with hybrid, prioritize by risk, build agility.
