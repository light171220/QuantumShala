## Summary

PQC research frontiers include:

1. **Efficiency**: Faster operations, smaller sizes
2. **Advanced Protocols**: Threshold, ZK, FHE, blind signatures
3. **Theory**: Tighter proofs, QROM security, new assumptions
4. **Implementation**: Side-channel protection, formal verification
5. **Open Problems**: Exact security, smaller signatures, new constructions

Active research ensures PQC will continue improving beyond initial standards.
